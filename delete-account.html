Account Deletion

Delete Your SCSK Account

Last updated: 15 December 2025

Permanent Action

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose access to all synced projects, jobs, photos, and data stored in your account. Local data on your device will remain unless you uninstall the app.

Request Deletion

You can request account deletion using the form below or by emailing us directly. We may need to verify account ownership before processing your request.

What Happens to Your Data

Permanently Deleted

  • Account profile & credentials
  • Synced projects & jobs
  • Cloud-stored photos
  • Contacts & notes
  • Calendar events
  • App preferences & settings

May Be Retained

  • Basic server logs (security)
  • Anonymised analytics
  • Legal compliance records
  • Fraud prevention data

Retained only when legally required

Processing Timeline

Typical deletion process
1 Request received
2 Ownership verified (1-2 days)
3 Data deleted (within 7 days)
4 Confirmation email sent

Most requests are processed within 7 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation once your account and data have been permanently deleted.
