Delete your SCSK account

Last updated: 15 December 2025

Important: Account deletion is permanent. You will lose access to any synced data and content stored in your account.

How to request deletion

Submit the form below or email us directly. We may verify ownership before processing.

Email request directly

What data is deleted

We aim to permanently delete:

  • Your account profile and sign-in identifiers
  • Synced project, job, and photo data (where applicable)
  • Any attachments stored in your account

What data may be retained

Limited data may be kept only when required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons (e.g., basic logs).

Timing

We process requests as quickly as possible — usually within a few days after verification.
