Account deletion

Delete your SCSK account

Last updated: 15 December 2025

Important: Account deletion is permanent. After deletion, you may lose access to synced data and any content stored in your account.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your Site Companion Side Kick (SCSK) account, submit the form below or email us directly. We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before completing the request.

Email deletion request

What data is deleted

When we complete an account deletion request, we aim to delete:

  • Your account profile and identifiers used for sign-in (where applicable)
  • Synced project/job data stored in your account (where applicable)
  • Synced photos/attachments stored in your account (where applicable)

What data may be retained

We may retain limited information only when necessary, such as:

  • Records required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes
  • Technical logs (e.g., crash/diagnostic logs) for a limited period
  • Information you have already exported, shared, or emailed outside the app (you control those copies)

Timing

We aim to process deletion requests as soon as possible. In some cases, deletion may take time to complete depending on verification and system processing.
