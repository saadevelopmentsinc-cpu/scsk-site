FAQ

Common Questions

How does offline mode work?

SCSK stores all data locally. Everything works without internet. Changes sync when you're back online.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Data is encrypted on device and during sync. We never sell your information.

What's included in the beta?

Full access to all features: projects, jobs, photos, contacts, PDF reports, calculators.

Will it work on my phone?

SCSK runs on Android 8.0+. Works on phones, tablets, and foldables.

How much does it cost?

Beta is free. We're gathering feedback before finalising pricing.

When is iOS coming?

Android first, then Windows. iOS is planned. Join beta to vote on priorities.
