A site companion built for real job sites.

Site Companion Side Kick (SCSK) is an offline-first companion app for supervisors, foremen, and project managers who need reliable workflows on active construction sites — even without internet access.

Designed for phones and tablets, SCSK syncs across devices when online and continues to evolve with weekly improvements driven by real site feedback.

What SCSK helps you do

Core functionality is already live in the beta and designed for practical, on-site use.

Projects & jobs

Organise work into structured projects and job folders with timestamps.

Photos & annotations

Capture photos on site and annotate issues directly.

Trade assignments

Assign jobs and defects to trades with notes and accountability.

PDF reporting
