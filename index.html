Core Features

Built for Real Job Sites

Practical tools designed for day-to-day construction site management. No corporate bloat, just features that work.

Smart Dashboard

Your command center. See project stats, pending jobs, weather, and quick actions at a glance. Customizable widgets show what matters to you.

Project Management

Complete project overview with jobs, photos, contacts, notes, files, and materials tracking. Everything organised in one place.

Multi-Project View

Manage unlimited projects. Filter by status, search instantly, see progress at a glance. Perfect for supervisors managing multiple sites.

Multi-Day Calendar

Day, week, and month views with multi-day job spanning like Google Calendar. Drag to reschedule. Color-coded by trade category.

Trade Calculators

15+ built-in calculators: tile, grout, paint, concrete, brick, waterproofing, electrical, pipe sizing, stairs, and more being added weekly.

Photo Documentation

Capture, annotate with arrows/text/drawings, and organise photos by job. Export professional photo reports as PDF. Works 100% offline.

Materials Tracking

Track materials per job with quantities, units, suppliers, and costs. Material templates for quick entry. Sync materials across projects.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about SCSK.

How does offline mode work?

SCSK stores all data locally first. Everything works without internet √¢‚Ç¨‚Äù create jobs, take photos, add notes. When you're back online, changes sync automatically in the background.

How often are updates released?

We release new features and improvements every week. Bug fixes come even faster. Beta users get updates via Google Play as soon as they're ready.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Data is encrypted on your device and during sync. We use Firebase (Google infrastructure) with enterprise-grade security. We never sell your data.

What's included in the beta?

Full access to all features: unlimited projects, jobs, photos, contacts, materials, notes, PDF reports, and all calculators. No feature restrictions.

Will it work on my device?

Android 8.0+ required. Optimised for tablets but works great on phones and foldables. Windows coming Q1 2026, iOS planned for later 2026.

How much will it cost?

Beta is free. We're finalising pricing based on feedback. Beta users will get special founding member pricing when we launch paid tiers.

Can I request features?

Absolutely! Email [email protected] or use the in-app feedback. Beta user requests directly influence our weekly development priorities.

When is cloud sync coming?

Multi-device cloud sync is our top priority for Q1 2026. It's in active development right now and will be released in stages.

Can my team use it?

Currently single-user. Team accounts with shared projects and permissions are planned for Q2-Q3 2026. Join beta to be first in line.

Who's building this?

SCSK is built by a solo developer who's also a working site foreman. Real construction experience, real problems, practical solutions.
