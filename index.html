FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about SCSK and the beta program.

How does offline mode work?

SCSK stores all your data locally on your device. Create projects, add jobs, take photos ‚Äî everything works without internet. When you're back online, changes sync automatically.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Data is encrypted both on your device and during sync. We never sell your information. You control what syncs to the cloud and can export or delete your data anytime.

What's included in the beta?

Full access to all current features including projects, jobs, photo documentation, contacts, PDF reports, and trade calculators. Beta users also get early access to new features.

Will it work on my phone?

SCSK runs on Android 8.0 (Oreo) and newer. Optimised for phones, tablets, and foldable devices. Designed to work smoothly on mid-range devices common on job sites.

How much does it cost?

The beta is free for approved testers. We're gathering feedback before finalising pricing. Beta testers will receive special consideration when we launch commercially.

When is iOS coming?

We're focused on perfecting Android first, with Windows desktop next. iOS is planned but not yet scheduled. Join the beta to vote on platform priorities.
