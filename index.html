Core Features

Built for Real Job Sites

Practical tools designed for day-to-day construction site management. No corporate bloat, just features that work.

Smart Dashboard

Your command center. See project stats, pending jobs, weather, and quick actions at a glance. Customizable widgets show what matters to you.

Project Management

Complete project overview with jobs, date ranges, photos, contacts, project-linked notes, files, and materials tracking. Everything organised in one place.

Multi-Project View

Manage unlimited projects. Filter by status, search instantly, see progress at a glance. Perfect for supervisors managing multiple sites.

Multi-Day Calendar

Day, week, and month views with multi-day job spanning like Google Calendar. Drag to reschedule. Color-coded by trade category.

Trade Calculators

16+ built-in calculators including tile adhesive, sand and cement bag counts, tile, grout, paint, concrete, brick, waterproofing, electrical, pipe sizing, stairs, and more.

Photo Documentation

Capture, annotate with arrows/text/drawings, and organise photos by job. Export professional photo reports as PDF. Works 100% offline.

Materials & Inventory

Track warehouse stock, suppliers, and grouped job allocations. Select inventory materials inside projects, adjust quantities inline, and spot low, critical, and out-of-stock items quickly.

Project Checklists

Use built-in residential, commercial, and renovation templates or create custom checklists. Track progress, notes, custom items, and not-applicable items per project.

Team Management

Invite team members, send email invites, and manage owner, admin, read/write, and read-only access. Pending invites are checked on launch for existing accounts.

Cross-Device Sync

Automatic launch sync keeps projects, notes, documents, plans, contacts, materials, and queued app data moving between Android and Windows when signed into the same account.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about SCSK.

How does offline mode work?

SCSK stores all data locally first. Everything works without internet — create jobs, take photos, add notes. When you're back online, changes sync automatically in the background.

How often are updates released?

We release new features and improvements every week. Bug fixes come even faster. Beta users get updates via Google Play as soon as they're ready.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Data is encrypted on your device and during sync. We use Firebase (Google infrastructure) with enterprise-grade security. We never sell your data.

What's included in the beta?

Full access to project and job management, photos and annotations, documents and plans, checklists, inventory, contacts, team permissions, PDF reports, and trade calculators.

Will it work on my device?

Android 6.0+ is supported for phones, tablets, and foldables. Windows 10 and Windows 11 are active in beta. iOS and iPadOS are in progress.

How much will it cost?

Beta is free. We're finalising pricing based on feedback. Beta users will get special founding member pricing when we launch paid tiers.

Can I request features?

Absolutely! Email [email protected] or use the in-app feedback. Beta user requests directly influence our weekly development priorities.

Does sync work across devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same account to sync projects, jobs, photos, documents, plans, contacts, materials, notes, and checklist data across supported devices. Sync reliability is still being hardened during beta.

Can my team use it?

Yes. Team management is available with owner, admin, editor, and viewer roles, plus member invite, suspend, reactivate, remove, and read/write permission controls.

Who's building this?

SCSK is built by a solo developer who's also a working site foreman. Real construction experience, real problems, practical solutions.
