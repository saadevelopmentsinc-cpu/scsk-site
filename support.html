FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about SCSK.

1 How does offline mode work?

Everything — projects, jobs, photos, notes — works fully offline. All data is stored locally on your device. When you get signal, data syncs automatically and safely to the cloud.

2 Can I use SCSK on multiple devices?

Yes! Sign in with the same account and your data syncs across all your phones and tablets. Changes made on one device appear on others when connected.

3 How do I get beta access?

Apply on the home page — we manually review each application to ensure you're a good fit. Approved users receive access instructions via email, usually within 24-48 hours.

4 Is my site data secure?

Yes — we use local-first storage with optional cloud sync, Firebase authentication, and encrypted data transfer. We never sell your data. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

5 What devices are supported?

SCSK runs on Android 8.0 (Oreo) and newer. It's optimised for phones, tablets, and foldable devices. Windows desktop version is coming soon.

6 How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion through the app settings or via our account deletion page. We'll process your request and confirm via email.

7 Can I export my data?

Yes! SCSK allows you to export projects as PDF reports, and you can request a full data export by contacting our support team.

8 Is there a cost during beta?

The beta is completely free for approved testers. We're gathering feedback before finalising pricing. Beta testers will receive special consideration when we launch commercially.
