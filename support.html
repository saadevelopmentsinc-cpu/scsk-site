Common questions

How does offline mode work?

Everything — projects, jobs, photos, notes — works fully offline. When you get signal, data syncs automatically and safely.

Can I use SCSK on multiple devices?

Yes! Sign in with the same account and your data syncs across phones and tablets.

How do I get beta access?

Apply on the home page — we review manually and send access instructions quickly.

Is my site data secure?

Yes — local-first storage, optional sync, Firebase auth, and no data selling. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
