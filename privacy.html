Privacy Policy — Site Companion Side Kick (SCSK)

Last updated: 15 December 2025

Plain-English summary:
  • SCSK is designed for professional site workflows.
  • Permissions are only used when you enable features that require them.
  • Your data is not sold.
  • Syncing across devices is optional.

1) Information we collect

  • Account identifiers (for sign-in and syncing)
  • Project, job, checklist, and defect data you enter
  • Photos, documents, and annotations you create
  • Optional audio notes or transcripts
  • Optional location data for geofencing prompts
  • Basic diagnostics to improve stability

2) How information is used

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Enable syncing across devices when turned on
  • Generate reports and exports you request
  • Improve performance and reliability

3) Sharing

Information may be shared only with infrastructure providers required to operate the service, or when you choose to export or share content. We do not sell personal data.

4) Security

Reasonable safeguards are used to protect information, but no system is completely secure.

5) Your choices

  • Manage permissions in device settings
  • Export or delete content you create
  • Contact support for data or access requests

6) Children’s privacy

SCSK is intended for professional use and is not directed at children.

7) Contact

  • Email: admin@sc-sk.com
  • Website: https://sc-sk.com
