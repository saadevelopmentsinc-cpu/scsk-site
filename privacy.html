1. Scope & Application

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") governs the collection, use, storage, and disclosure of personal information by SAA ("we," "us," or "our") in connection with the Site Companion Side Kick mobile application ("SCSK" or the "App") and any related services, websites, or platforms we operate.

By downloading, installing, or using SCSK, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree with any aspect of this Policy, please do not use our services.

This Policy applies to all users of SCSK, including construction professionals, tradespeople, project managers, and any other individuals who interact with our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, maintain, and improve SCSK's functionality. The types of information we collect depend on how you use the App and the features you choose to enable.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and authentication credentials. You may optionally provide additional profile details such as your trade specialty, company name, or profile photo.
  • Project & Job Data: Information you enter about your construction projects, including project names, addresses, client details, job descriptions, task lists, schedules, and status updates.
  • Contact Information: Details of clients, subcontractors, suppliers, and other contacts you add to the App, including names, phone numbers, email addresses, and company affiliations.
  • Media Content: Photos, documents, drawings, annotations, and any other files you capture or upload through the App to document your work on site.
  • Notes & Communications: Quick notes, voice memos, transcriptions, and any text-based content you create within the App.
  • Calculations & Measurements: Data entered into our construction calculators, including dimensions, quantities, and material specifications.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, app version, and general hardware specifications necessary for compatibility and support.
  • Usage Analytics: Information about how you interact with the App, including features used, screens viewed, session duration, and interaction patterns. This data is collected in aggregate form to improve the user experience.
  • Crash & Performance Data: Technical diagnostics including crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics to identify and resolve issues that affect app stability.
  • Network Information: Connection type (WiFi, mobile data) and sync status to optimise offline functionality and data synchronisation.

2.3 Information Collected with Your Permission

  • Location Data: With your explicit consent, we may collect precise or approximate location data to enable features such as geofencing reminders, site-based task prompts, and location tagging for photos. You can enable or disable location access at any time through your device settings.
  • Camera & Microphone Access: Required only when you choose to capture photos, record voice notes, or use related documentation features. Access is requested at the point of use.
  • Calendar Integration: If you choose to sync with your device calendar, we access calendar data solely to display and coordinate scheduling information within the App.
  • Sensor Data: Compass and accelerometer data when using our level and orientation tools, processed locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Core Service Delivery

  • Providing and operating SCSK's features and functionality
  • Enabling project management, job tracking, and documentation capabilities
  • Generating reports, exports, and summaries you request
  • Synchronising your data across devices when you enable cloud sync
  • Processing calculations and providing construction-related tools

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analysing usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Identifying and fixing bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Developing new features based on user needs and feedback
  • Optimising app performance for construction site conditions

3.3 Communication

  • Sending service-related notifications and updates
  • Responding to your support requests and inquiries
  • Providing important security or account-related alerts
  • Delivering optional feature announcements (with your consent)

3.4 Security & Compliance

  • Protecting against fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service and other policies
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations
  • Preserving evidence for potential legal proceedings when required

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing required to provide you with SCSK's services as outlined in our Terms of Service.
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as improving our services, ensuring security, and preventing fraud, balanced against your privacy rights.
  • Consent: Where required by law, we obtain your explicit consent before collecting or processing certain types of information, such as precise location data.
  • Legal Obligations: Processing necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

5. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party service providers to perform functions on our behalf, including:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Secure data storage and synchronisation services
  • Authentication Services: Account verification and security
  • Analytics Providers: Anonymised usage analysis to improve the App
  • Customer Support Platforms: Tools to help us respond to your inquiries

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to perform services on our behalf.

5.2 Your Explicit Sharing

When you choose to export, share, or transmit data from the App (such as generating a report to send to a client), you control that sharing and the recipients you select.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of SAA, our users, or the public
  • Detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues
  • Respond to lawful requests from public authorities

5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganisation, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change and any choices you may have regarding your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfil the purposes described in this Policy. Specific retention periods include:

  • Active Account Data: Retained while your account remains active and for a reasonable period thereafter to allow for reactivation.
  • Project & Job Data: Retained until you delete it or close your account, plus any legally required retention period.
  • Usage Analytics: Retained in aggregate, anonymised form indefinitely for service improvement purposes.
  • Backup Data: May persist in backup systems for up to 90 days after deletion from active systems.
  • Legal Compliance: Certain information may be retained longer if required by law or to protect our legal interests.

You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements and our legitimate business interests.

7. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

7.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL protocols and at rest using industry-standard encryption algorithms.
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorisation mechanisms limit access to your data.
  • Secure Infrastructure: We utilise reputable cloud providers with robust security certifications and practices.
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing of our systems.

7.2 Operational Safeguards

  • Employee access to personal data is limited to those who require it for their job functions
  • Staff are trained on privacy and security best practices
  • Incident response procedures are in place to address potential breaches

Important: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use our services at your own risk. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have several rights and options regarding your personal information:

8.1 Access & Portability

You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. Where technically feasible, we can provide this in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

8.2 Correction

You can update or correct your account information directly within the App, or contact us to request corrections to other personal data.

8.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal information. We will comply with such requests unless we have a legitimate reason or legal obligation to retain certain data. You can also delete individual projects, jobs, photos, and other content directly within the App.

8.4 Restrict Processing

In certain circumstances, you may request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.

8.5 Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on your consent for processing, you may withdraw that consent at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing conducted prior to withdrawal.

8.6 Device Permissions

You can control app permissions (camera, location, microphone, etc.) through your device's settings at any time. Disabling certain permissions may limit corresponding features.

8.7 Communications Preferences

You can opt out of promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in those messages or adjusting your notification settings within the App.

8.8 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided in Section 13. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

9. International Data Transfers

SCSK is operated from Australia. If you access our services from outside Australia, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Australia or other countries where our service providers operate.

When transferring data internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards to ensure your information receives an adequate level of protection, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by relevant authorities
  • Data processing agreements with service providers
  • Compliance with applicable cross-border data transfer frameworks

10. Children's Privacy

SCSK is designed for professional construction use and is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe we may have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.
