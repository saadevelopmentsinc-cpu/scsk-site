Privacy Policy — Site Companion Side Kick (SCSK)

Last updated: 15 December 2025

Plain-English summary:
  • SCSK is your practical daily companion on construction sites.
  • We only request permissions when you use features that need them.
  • Your site data is never sold.
  • Syncing across devices is optional and controlled by you.

1) Information we collect

  • Account identifiers (for sign-in and optional syncing)
  • Project, job, and task data you enter
  • Photos, documents, and annotations you create on site
  • Optional voice notes or transcripts
  • Optional location data for geofencing prompts
  • Basic diagnostics to keep the app stable

2) How information is used

  • To deliver core app features on site
  • To enable optional multi-device sync
  • To generate the reports you request
  • To improve reliability for real-world use

3) Sharing

We only share data with essential infrastructure providers or when you explicitly export/share it yourself. We do not sell personal data.

4) Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, though no system is 100% secure.

5) Your choices

  • Control permissions in your device settings
  • Export or delete your own content anytime
  • Contact support for data requests

6) Children’s privacy

SCSK is built for professional construction use and is not intended for children.
