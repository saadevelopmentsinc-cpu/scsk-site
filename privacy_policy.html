Privacy Policy

Site Companion Side Kick (SCSK) · Last updated: 14 December 2025

1. Introduction

Site Companion Side Kick (SCSK) is a site companion and audit application developed by SAA. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the SCSK mobile application on Android (and future platforms).

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you sign in or create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Authentication identifiers (via Firebase or similar services)

This is used solely to authenticate you and sync your data across devices.

2.2 Project & Job Data (User-Generated Content)

You may voluntarily create and store:

  • Projects and job folders
  • Notes and comments
  • Checklists (safety, compliance, quality, etc.)
  • Assigned trades and contact details
  • Dates and times of audits
  • Attached plans or documents
  • Generated reports (PDFs)

This data belongs to you.

2.3 Photos & Media

SCSK allows you to take photos within jobs, annotate images, and attach images to reports.

Photos are stored:

  • Locally on your device, and
  • Synced to your account only if cloud sync is enabled

We do not access your photos outside of app functionality.

2.4 Location Data (Optional)

If enabled by you, SCSK may collect approximate or precise location data and geofencing information.

Location data is used only to:

  • Associate jobs with locations
  • Prompt you when you return to a known site

You can disable location access at any time via device settings.

2.5 Speech-to-Text & Audio Notes (Optional)

If you use voice input, audio may be temporarily processed to convert speech to text. Audio is not permanently stored unless you explicitly save it.

Processing may rely on device or trusted third-party speech services.

2.6 AI-Assisted Features (Future)

Future versions of SCSK may include AI tools to identify potential non-compliance in photos and assist with report summaries.

If enabled:

  • Images may be processed securely
  • AI analysis is limited to app functionality
  • Data is not used to train public AI models
Note: You control optional permissions such as location, camera, microphone, and cloud sync. You can change these settings at any time in your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Store and sync your projects and jobs
  • Generate reports and PDFs
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Support offline usage and later syncing
  • Comply with legal requirements

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored locally on your device where possible
  • Cloud sync uses secure, encrypted storage
  • Industry-standard security practices are applied
  • Access is restricted to authenticated users

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Offline Use

SCSK is designed to work offline:

  • Data entered offline remains on your device
  • Sync occurs only when an internet connection is available
  • You control when syncing is enabled

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • Essential service providers (e.g. authentication, cloud storage)
  • When required by law

Trades only receive information you intentionally include in shared reports.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Edit or delete your projects and jobs
  • Disable permissions (camera, location, microphone)
  • Request account deletion

Account deletion removes associated cloud data.

8. Children’s Privacy

SCSK is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated “Last updated” date.
